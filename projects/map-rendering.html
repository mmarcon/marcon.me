Map Rendering

Experiments with OpenStreetMap data and different ways of rendering maps.

March 2016

Programming/Scripting

I have been working on maps and location for more than 4 years, but I realized I have never tried to render my own maps.

I spent a few hours with Mapnik. Here is one of the final maps. I tried rendering the streets of different cities, states and countries and the streets of Florida turned out to be the prettiest result.

Even though I make maps for a living, I've actually never tried rendering maps from raw data. Here's my first attempt, Florida. #map #florida #sunshinestate
